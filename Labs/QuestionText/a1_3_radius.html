PROBLEM STATEMENT:

You are trying to write a program that calculates the volume of multiple identical cylinders. The template provided to you defines 3 variables:

  • radius - radius of the cylinders
  • height- height of the cylinders
  • total - The total amount of cylinders

 

Calculate the volume, using the following formula:

V = π r2 h t

Where . . .

    • π is the mathematical constant (approximately equal to 3.14... -- see the HINT for more details)
    • r is the radius of the cylinder
    • h is the height of the cylinder
    • t is the total amount of cylinders

 

Store the answer in a variable called volume. You do not need to print the variable.

 

HINT:

The math module contains the mathematical constant, π. To use it, simply type math.pi
