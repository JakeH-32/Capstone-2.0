PROBLEM STATEMENT:

You are trying to write a program that calculates the amount of money a person will earn from an investment. The template provided to you defines 3 variables:

  • principal_amount - The principal (i.e., starting) amount of money that a person has
  • interest_rate - The interest rate
  • time - The length of the investment (in months)

 

Calculate the amount of money earned, using the following formula:

amount = pert

Where . . .

    • p is the principal amount
    • e is the mathematical constant (approximately equal to 2.718... -- see the HINT for more details)
    • r is the interest rate
    • t is the time

 

Store the answer in a variable called amount. You do not need to print the variable.

 

HINT:

The math module contains the mathematical constant, e. To use it, simply type math.e
