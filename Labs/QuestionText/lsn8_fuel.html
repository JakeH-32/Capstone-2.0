PROBLEM STATEMENT:

Write an algorithm that gets a fuel load (in lbs) and then a distance to base (in nautical miles). You may assume all inputs are non-negative numbers.

It then outputs what kind of action the pilot should take. If the fuel load is less than 50 lbs and the distance to base is less than 70 nm, report “Return to base”, otherwise, report “Resume flight”.
