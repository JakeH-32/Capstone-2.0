PROBLEM STATEMENT:

This lab makes use of the sat.csv dataset, which describes the average SAT performance for students attending 350+ schools. Here is a typical line from the file:

 

Henry Street School for International Studies, 391, 425, 385

 


Each position in the line (separated by a comma) represents a different "data element". Here is a key for your reference:

School Name Critical Reading Mean Mathematics Mean Writing Mean
Henry Street School for International Studies 391 425 385



Write a Python algorithm that calculates the average SAT score across all schools, and outputs the names of every school that is below the average.

 

NOTES (really really important):

  • You can get a school's average score by adding up the critical reading, mathematics, and writing composite scores.
