PROBLEM STATEMENT:

You are trying to write a program that calculates the area of multiple circles. The template provided to you defines 2 variables:

  • radius - radius of the circle
  • total - The total amount of circles

 

Calculate the volume, using the following formula:

V=π r2t

Where . . .

    • π is the mathematical constant (approximately equal to 3.14... -- see the HINT for more details)
    • r is the radius of the circle
    • t is the total amount of circles

 

Store the answer in a variable called area. You do not need to print the variable.

 

HINT:

The math module contains the mathematical constant, π. To use it, simply type math.pi
