PROBLEM STATEMENT:

You are trying to write a program that calculates the volume of multiple identical cones. The template provided to you defines 3 variables:

  • radius - radius of the cones
  • height- height of the cones
  • total - The total amount of cones

 

Calculate the volume, using the following formula:

V = π r2(h/3)t

Where . . .

    • π is the mathematical constant (approximately equal to 3.14... -- see the HINT for more details)
    • r is the radius of the cones
    • h is the height of the cones
    • t is the total amount of cones

 

Store the answer in a variable called volume. You do not need to print the variable.

 

HINT:

The math module contains the mathematical constant, e. To use it, simply type math.pi
